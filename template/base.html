How CivicBridge Works

Simple process from reporting to resolution

1

Report an Issue

Take a photo, add details, and pinpoint the location of the civic issue you've encountered.

2

Auto-Assignment

Our system automatically routes your complaint to the relevant department based on category and location.

3

Track Progress

Monitor your complaint status in real-time with notifications at every stage of the process.

4

Department Action

Authorities review, prioritize, and work on resolving the reported issues.

5

Resolution & Verification

Once resolved, officers upload proof and the system notifies you for confirmation.

6

Auto-Escalation

If unresolved within timeframe, complaint automatically escalates to higher authorities.
